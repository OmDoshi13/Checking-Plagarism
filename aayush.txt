The management of supply chains is vital for business success. It encompasses planning, procurement, manufacturing, and distribution to enhance operational efficiency. Many organizations strive to optimize their supply chains using advanced technologies.

A major component of supply chain efficiency is data analysis. Studying historical trends and market demands allows businesses to make better decisions. Moreover, AI and ML are revolutionizing demand forecasting by improving accuracy.

Sustainability is an increasingly important factor in supply chains. Companies are adopting eco-friendly strategies to minimize their environmental impact.
