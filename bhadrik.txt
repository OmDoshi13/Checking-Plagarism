Efficient supply chain management is essential for businesses to remain competitive. The process includes planning, sourcing, production, and delivering products efficiently. Many companies integrate modern technologies to streamline their supply chain operations.

One of the most important aspects of supply chain improvement is data-driven decision-making. By evaluating previous trends and market conditions, businesses can optimize their operations. AI and ML have significantly enhanced predictive analytics in supply chain forecasting.

Environmental sustainability is now a major focus in supply chain management. Businesses are implementing greener logistics strategies to reduce carbon emissions.
